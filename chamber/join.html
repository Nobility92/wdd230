Membership Level

This level is for Non-profit organizations and they are free of charge. They as well get all the whole benefit.

Bronze Membership gives you access to special monthly training events and 2 round table seat held by the chamber.

Cost: $15/yr

Silver membership gives you access to special monthly training events, 5 round table seat plus free advertizing on our homepage.

Cost: $25/yr

Gold membership gives you access to special monthly training events, 10 round table seat plus free advertizing on our homepage and events.

Cost: $35/yr
